Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

Sweet and sticky, this Honey Garlic Chicken is the perfect quick weeknight meal. Tender cubes of boneless chicken are stir-fried in a delicious honey garlic & ginger sauce. Served over rice or alongside steamed veggies, this is a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortlilas
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Refried Beans
  • Ortega Sauce
